How it all started ...

This craft brewery is the result of 3 friends that were curious about their favorite drink : beer. Two years ago, when i started my apprenticeship I got the chance to meet some craft brewers and after talking to them they convinced me to try it myslef in my own kitchen. So off we went and bought our first brewing kit and started brewing some pretty basic beer. After a few trials & errors we eventually understood the process and started to make good quality beer. That's when we decided to upgrade our brewing equipment, we did a small crowdfundning & started a real brewery. We are now located in western Paris near Saint Germain en Laye and we procduce 50L batches that we sell beer to our familly & friends.
